About Us

We are your one-stop store for home made meals, just Call-a-Bowl and we are right at your doorstep.

CallaBowl is a walk-in and online restaurant serving & delivering delicious and hygienically prepared meals & soup bowls; we cater for lunch packs, family meals, intimate parties and medium outdoor event. We're located in the heart of Lekki Phase 1, right in front of the IMAX FilmHouse Cinema.

HOW TO ORDER

  1. Pick your meal, please add the cost of bowls
  2. Click on Order Now button
  3. Make payment into the bank account
  4. Click on Complete Order button which takes you to our whatsapp chat
  5. Send proof of payment & delivery address via WhatsApp
  6. Please note that delivery charges will be communicated to you and are payable directly to the rider
